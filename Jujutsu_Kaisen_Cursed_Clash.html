Game Description

Jujutsu Kaisen: Cursed Clash is a 2v2 arena fighting game developed by Byking and published by Bandai Namco Entertainment, bringing the supernatural action and teamwork of the Jujutsu Kaisen anime and manga series to life. Players can choose from over fifteen characters, including fan-favorites like Yuji Itadori, Megumi Fushiguro, Nobara Kugisaki, and Satoru Gojo, each equipped with unique cursed techniques and moves. The game emphasizes teamwork and strategy, allowing players to mix and match character skills to create powerful combos, awaken abilities, and perform ultimate moves in dynamic environments.


With vibrant visuals, smooth combat mechanics, and a faithful representation of the anime’s style, Jujutsu Kaisen: Cursed Clash offers both solo and co-op modes, where players can engage in battles against AI or test their skills against others online. The game combines fast-paced, supernatural action with interactive environments, creating an immersive experience for both dedicated fans and newcomers to the Jujutsu Kaisen universe.

Game Story


Jujutsu Kaisen: Cursed Clash follows the main storyline of Jujutsu Kaisen, where Yuji Itadori, a high school student with extraordinary physical abilities, finds himself thrust into the dangerous world of jujutsu sorcery after consuming a powerful cursed object. The game begins with Yuji’s introduction to the Tokyo Jujutsu High School, where he trains under powerful sorcerers like Satoru Gojo and teams up with classmates Megumi and Nobara to learn the ways of jujutsu sorcery and to fight against evil curses that threaten humanity.


As players progress, they experience key battles and story arcs from the anime, including confrontations with powerful curses like Mahito and Sukuna. Jujutsu Kaisen: Cursed Clash explores themes of friendship, sacrifice, and the struggle against fate, with gameplay and cutscenes that highlight Yuji’s growth as a sorcerer and the bonds he forms with his allies. The game’s story mode captures the intense battles and emotional depth of the Jujutsu Kaisen series, letting players immerse themselves in its dark, supernatural world.
