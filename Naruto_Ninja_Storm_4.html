Game Description

Naruto Shippuden: Ultimate Ninja Storm 4 is a fast-paced, action-packed fighting game developed by CyberConnect2 and published by Bandai Namco Entertainment. This final installment in the Ultimate Ninja Storm series brings the intense battles and storytelling of the Naruto universe to life, with refined combat mechanics, vibrant visuals, and interactive environments. It follows the later stages of the Naruto Shippuden storyline, allowing players to control a wide variety of fan-favorite characters, each with unique abilities and fighting styles, as they engage in high-speed battles.


The game offers both offline and online multiplayer modes, where players can master combos, awakenings, and ultimate jutsus to face off against others locally or online. With stunning cinematic visuals and dynamic environments that can be destroyed during battles, Naruto Storm 4 delivers a thrilling experience that appeals to both fans and newcomers alike, blending fast-paced combat with anime-inspired storytelling.

Game Story


The story mode in Naruto Shippuden: Ultimate Ninja Storm 4 focuses on the climactic Fourth Great Ninja War arc, diving directly into the intense battles between the Allied Shinobi Forces and the combined powers of the Ten-Tails, Madara Uchiha, and Obito Uchiha. Players experience key moments in the Naruto Shippuden storyline, including Naruto’s fight against the masked Obito, Sasuke’s return to the battlefield, and the ultimate confrontation with the god-like Kaguya Otsutsuki, all depicted in stunning cinematic sequences.


Throughout the story, players control various iconic characters from the series, such as Naruto, Sasuke, Kakashi, Madara, and Kaguya, each with their own fighting styles and ultimate moves. The story explores themes of friendship, sacrifice, and personal growth, with a particularly deep focus on Naruto and Sasuke as they confront their differences to fight for peace. Naruto Storm 4 provides an immersive experience of the anime’s final arc, combining emotional moments with intense, strategic combat, and allowing fans to relive the series’ climactic end.
